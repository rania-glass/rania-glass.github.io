Who am I?

Rania Glass

I study Computer Science at Georgia Tech. I regularly work with Java, Python, HTML5/CSS3, and Javascript. Additionally, I use GIMP (Photoshop for Linux), Adobe Dreamweaver, MS Office Suite, and vim on a regular basis.
I am a junior executive with Georgia Tech Design Club . My duties include representing Design Club to the GT College of Computing and to other peer organizations, helping to organize presentations for our weekly meetings, and implementing community building strategies among members of Design Club.
I enjoy studying psychology and marketing. Why do we like what we like? What makes a website or app easy to navigate, and why? What feelings can be elicited by using a particular color palette, or a specific grouping of content? These are the kinds of questions that I am interested in exploring.
I like exploring my city. I enjoy rock climbing, yoga, and spending time exploring quaint little coffeeshops in artsy neighborhoods.
